Our Solution
05 / 12

Parallax — perspective through encounter

Named for the shift in view when you change position. Parallax matches strangers with opposing stances on a curated topic for short, video-first conversations — Omegle's spontaneity with purpose and guardrails.

  • 10-question values survey → ideology profile (never labeled)
  • Topic picker → queue for ideological opposite on same issue
  • 1:1 video + text chat with skip, report, and time limits
  • AI moderation + Exa-powered factual context cards

~30s queue

Opposite matching, not echo matching

Omegle pairs shared interests. Parallax pairs maximum ideological distance on the topic's primary axis.

Icebreaker on connect

"You were matched because you see [topic] differently. Try to understand their view first."

Skip anytime

Instant re-queue — low friction keeps practice sessions frequent, like sparring.

Post-session feedback

"Did you better understand the other side?" — tracks our core success metric.

Matching logic: primary-axis distance scoring with 30s timeout and adjacent-bucket fallback · PRD § User Flow
Product Pillars
07 / 12

01 · Match

Ideological opposites on real topics

Eight Values–inspired survey. Topics tagged to economic, diplomatic, civil, or societal axes.

02 · Connect

Video-first 1:1 rooms

LiveKit WebRTC. Face-to-face humanizes disagreement — harder to dehumanize than text threads.

03 · Protect

AI moderation stack

Text + sampled video frames. Tiered actions: warn → end session → IP ban after 3 violations/24h.

04 · Ground

Exa fact-check cards

Verifiable claims only — neutral tone, cited sources. Opinions stay untouched; facts get context.
